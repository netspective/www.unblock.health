What is Unblock Health?

Unblock Health is the chief individual patient access solution and excuse eliminator.

Unblock Health is the premier solution for one of the biggest failures in health care: individual patient access services.

Our intelligently designed SaaS makes it easy for patients to request their health information and make correction requests in a digitized, structured, and comprehensive manner while empowering request fulfillers with streamlined workflows, all-in-one command centers, and Information Blocking Rule compliance tools.

UNBLOCK HEALTH FEATURES

Unblock Health replaces the status quo with features to ensure all individual access requests are responded to and fullfilled in a timely manner.

Elevate Communication, Build Trust

Bi-directional, Asynchronous Messaging

Can you imagine not being able to text or direct message with essential contacts all day? Power your individual access request services with our unique asynchronous messaging as a necessary real-world conduit for fast communication between requestors and fulfillers. All communication activities are centralized and documented. It's quick, simple, and effective.

Lead the Way with Industry Standards

Unblock Health is strategically designed using published best practices from the Office of the National Coordinator of Health IT (ONC), the American Medical Association (AMA), and The Sequoia Project.

Our SaaS solution bring the most essential best practices on information-sharing and Information Blocking compliance from print to digital life so your team can confidently deploy streamlined individual access services.

  • Digitalization is necessary, empower your organization today.
  • Demonstrate good intent with pioneering action.
