After more than 25 years of witnessing patients suffer with barriers in accessing their health information, the time had come to stop the suffering and build a solution..

Unblock Health is a FHIR native, fully integratable SaaS strategically designed to:

  • Propel outdated, fragmented individual patient access workflows to the future information sharing, creating a centralized funnel to capture all aspects of the individual access process.
  • Elevate transparency into individual access request processes through dual dashboards, real-time status and activity tracking, and asynchronous, bi-directional messaging.
  • Empower request fulfillers with simple Information Blocking Rule compliance documentation capabilities and intuitive reporting.
  • Enable organizations to be prepared to address medical record correction requests that typically follow access requests.
