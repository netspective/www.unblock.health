Why Unblock Health?

Unblock Health will reduce & eliminate unnecessary Patient Administrative Burden.

Unblock Health is SaaS first-in-class suite of patient-focused solutions designed to reduce Patient Administrative Burden (PAB), or the work that patients and their families need to do to get the care they need. Unblock Health is FHIR-enabled, ready to integrate, and customizable.

Health Information Management

UH is ready to help healthcare delivery organizations (HDOs) eliminate or reduce the potential for millions of dollars in fines that will start on October 6, 2022. Healthcare delivery organizations (HDOs), providers, and physicians can trust UH to

  • modernize and streamline their patient access workflows
  • support health information management (HIM) professionals and staff to work more efficiently, with greater accuracy, while reducing costs
  • satisfy HIPAA requirements, the regulations in the Information Blocking Rules, and 21st Century Cures Act.

Office of Patient Experience

Unblock Health provides a fresh approach in improving customer satisfaction and brand reputation by addressing Patient Administrative Burden, as well as providing direct value in:

  • improving patient on-boarding
  • increasing patient trust and satisfaction
  • improving patient care coordination
  • reducing risk of Information Blocking complaints, corresponding fines, and corrective action plans

Regulatory: HHS, ONC, CMS

While the public awaits HHS Secretary Xavier Becerra's office to define civil monetary penalties for providers, Secretary Xavier Becerra did comment that defining this enforcement was a top HHS priority during the annual ONC meeting, emphasizing the high number of complaints being filed against providers.

There are strong sentiments that providers should be held accountable to the same disincentives and penalties as health IT vendors and health information exchange (HIEs).
