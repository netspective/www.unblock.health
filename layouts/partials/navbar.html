Unblock Health is the EASY button every patient needs

Unblock Health provides a suite of digital tools that transform traditionally time-consuming tasks that patients and their families routinely perform into streamlined actions.

Unblock Health addresses the most common HDO customer service issues: frustrating, outdated patient request workflows, long request turnaround wait times, inability to connect with a human.

Unblock Health leverages the power of bi-directional, asynchronous messaging to directly connect patients with health information management (HIM) professionals, privacy officers, providers, billing, and finance departments.
